Frequently Asked Questions

There are a number of questions answered. You can find your answer or ask another question.

Ask a question

  • Who can use our service?

    You must be at least 18 years old to use Konsilmed. Please make sure you tell us your legal name and correct date of birth - if your name and DOB don’t match what’s on your photo ID, we may not be able to treat you. Please make sure to check out the FAQs for the specific service you’d like to use in order to verify any unique requirements or limitations.

  • Is the service private/secure?

    Yes. We have a legal duty to protect your personal health information and have put in place a number of safeguards. Please Read Our Privacy Policy details to know how we protect your personal information.

  • How can I let my doctor know about the care you provide?

    If you have a doctor you see on an ongoing basis, it's important to tell your doctor about all the medicines you're taking, including any medicines we prescribe. To make this convenient for you, you can e-mail yourself a summary of the care we've provided via our mobile app. You should give this e-mail to your doctor so that your health records are complete.

  • What is konsilmed?

    Konsilmed UG (Limited Liability Company) is a German limited liability company with its registered office at Mainzer Landstrasse 50, 60325 Frankfurt am Main. ("Konsilmed") to use the online advice at https://www.konsilmed.com/ and others at the portal https://www.konsilmed.com/ ("Online advice") Konsilmed does not offer medical advice and is not licensed as a doctor or in any other medical profession. Konsilmed only performs the technical service of the platform. Consultation services are provided by the participating doctors.

  • What is the content of the Konsilmed services?

    Konsilmed provides a technical platform that patients can use to get in touch with doctors. Patients can arrange and conduct online counseling with the doctors. The video consultation takes place directly between the patient via an encrypted connection. Konsilmed only provides the technical processing of the service.
